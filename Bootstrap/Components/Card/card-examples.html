The building block of a card is the .card-body. Use it whenever a padded section within a card is needed.

Card titles are used by adding .card-title to an <h*> tag. And subtitles are used by adding .card-subtitle to an <h*> tag as well. If the .card-title and .card-subtitle are placed together in a .card-body item, the card title and subtitle are aligned nicely.
